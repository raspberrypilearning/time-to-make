## What you will make

Make a spinning selection wheel with cardboard and a stepper motor. The selection wheel will  reveal one of four options each time it turns.

You can design your own selection wheel to help you in lots of different ways! For example, your selection wheel could:
- Ask quiz questions
- Give yes or no answers
- Generate story ideas
- Decide chores like who cooks

### You will need

**For the circuit:**
- Computer with the [Thonny](https://thonny.org/) IDE installed
- Raspberry Pi Pico and micro USB cable
- Breadboard
- Pin-to-pin and socket-to-pin wires
- Stepper motor and driver
- Button
- LED
- 220Î© resistor

![A button, resistor, LED, Raspberry Pi Pico, breadboard, stepper motor, and driver.](images/all-parts.png){:width="500px"}

**For the device:**
- Small cardboard box such as a shoebox, or cardboard to make a box
- Cardboard to make the wheel and two spacer circles
- Pencil
- Scissors
- Sticky tape
- Glue (optional)
- Materials to decorate (such as pens, paint, or coloured stickers or paper)
