## More challenges
--- task ---
Can you make the wheel do a little "dance" before stopping? 

Experiment with `stepper.turn()` to move the wheel. 
--- /task ---

--- task ---
Could you add a second LED to make it more lively?

**Tip**: you will need to define the second LED with a different variable name such as "LED2".
--- /task ---


--- task ---
Add another button to turn the wheel to a specific location.

**Tip:** use `stepper.turn_to` to define the angle you want to **turn to**. See [stepper motor documentation](https://picozero.readthedocs.io/en/latest/api.html#stepper){:target="_blank"}for the parameters you need. 

--- /task ---

--- task ---
Instead of turning randomly, get the wheel to stop depending on the time of day.

**Tip:** you could use `localtime` from the `time` library to help do this.
--- /task ---
